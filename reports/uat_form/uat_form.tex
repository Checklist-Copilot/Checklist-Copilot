\subsection*{About This Project}

Checkly is a student project created by a small group for one of our classes. We are inviting people to try it and share honest feedback so we can learn what works and what could be improved.

You're welcome to create your own account or, if you prefer not to, you may use our test account.\\
\textbf{Email:} \texttt{test@checkly.com} \qquad \textbf{Password:} \texttt{test}

\subsection*{What is Checkly?}

Checkly is an \textbf{AI-assisted checklist web platform} that makes it easier for you to create, manage, and complete checklists by integrating AI into every step of the process.
