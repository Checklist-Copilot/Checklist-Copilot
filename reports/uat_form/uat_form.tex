\section*{Testing Tasks}

\textbf{App link:} \url{https://checkly-builder.vercel.app/}

Checkly is an AI-assisted checklist tool: you create a checklist, optionally attach a PDF for context, and then fill it out --- checking off items, adding photos, and using an AI assistant to help build or complete it.

\textbf{Possible use cases} (pick whichever fits you, or invent your own): a safety inspection, an equipment/maintenance check, a quality audit, a site walkthrough, a recurring cleaning or setup routine, an onboarding/training checklist, or a packing/travel checklist.

Feel free to explore the app on your own. If you'd like some guidance, here are a few recommended steps:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.6em,itemsep=0.3em]
  \item \textbf{Sign up / log in} at the link above.
  \item \textbf{Create a new checklist} with a title and description.
  \item \textbf{Upload a PDF} as context for the checklist.
  \item \textbf{Build out the checklist}: add a section and a mix of item types (checkbox, text, photo).
  \item \textbf{Use the AI Assistant} to add or modify something in the checklist.
  \item \textbf{Run through the checklist}: check off items, fill in a text field, and upload a photo as evidence.
  \item \textbf{Check the dashboard} for progress / stats on the checklist.
  \item \textbf{Export the checklist to PDF}.
\end{enumerate}
