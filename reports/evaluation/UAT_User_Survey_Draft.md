# Checkly — User Acceptance Testing Survey

Thank you for taking the time to try out **Checkly**! 🙏

Checkly is an AI-assisted checklist tool: you design a checklist (e.g. a safety inspection, equipment check, or quality audit), optionally attach reference documents (PDFs) for context, and then fill it out — verifying items with photos and an AI assistant that can help you build and check off the form by chatting with it.

We're building this as part of a university project and would love your honest feedback — including the negative kind! It helps us much more than compliments.

## Part 2 — Try Checkly

Please complete these steps in order. Don't worry about doing it "perfectly" — we want to see how it feels for a first-time user.

1. **Sign up / log in** at [INSERT LINK].
2. **Create a new checklist** — give it a title and description (e.g. "Safety inspection — Warehouse A").
3. **Upload a PDF as context** for the checklist (use the sample PDF above, or one of your own, e.g. a guideline/manual).
4. **Build out the checklist**: add at least one section, and a mix of item types (e.g. a checkbox, a text field, and a photo-check item).
5. **Open the AI Assistant** and ask it to do at least one thing for you — e.g. *"Add a section for fire safety with two checkbox items"* or *"What does this checklist cover?"*
6. **Run through the checklist as if performing the inspection**: check off a few items, fill in a text field, and upload at least one photo as evidence.
7. **Go to the dashboard** and check your progress / stats for the checklist you created.
8. **Export the checklist to PDF** using the "Create PDF" option.
