\subsection*{What is Checkly?}

Checkly is an AI-assisted checklist web platform that makes it easier for you to create, manage, and complete checklists by integrating AI into every step of the process.

\subsection*{Example Use Cases}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.4em,itemsep=0.2em]
  \item Safety inspection
  \item Equipment and maintenance check
  \item Quality audit
  \item Site walkthrough
  \item Recurring task
  \item Setup routine
  \item Onboarding and training checklist
  \item Packing and travel checklist
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Your Task}

Feel free to explore the app on your own. If youŌĆÖd like some guidance, here are a few recommended steps:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.4em,itemsep=0.25em]
  \item Sign up / log in
  \item Create a new checklist
  \item Build out the checklist: add a section and a mix of item types (checkbox, text, photo)
  \item Use the AI Assistant to add or modify something in the checklist
  \item Complete the checklist by filling in its items
\end{itemize}
